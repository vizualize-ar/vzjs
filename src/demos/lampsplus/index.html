Description

Combining matte black with warm antique brass finish, this pin-up wall lamp is a perfect addition to an elegant home office.

Additional Info:

Upgrade the wall lighting in a bedroom or home office with this elegant pin-up wall lamp from 360 Lighting. Inspired by Mid-Century modern design, the light features a downward pointing cone shade crafted in metal, in a wonderful matte black finish with antique brass accents. The light pivots up, down, left and right, however, the arm is stationary.

  • 11 1/2" high x 6 1/4" wide. Extends maximum 12 1/2" from the wall. Square backplate is 5" wide x 1" deep.
  • Shade is 2 3/4" across the top x 6 1/4" across the bottom x 8 3/4" high. 8-foot long clear silver cord. Cord cover is not included.
  • Uses one maximum 40 watt standard-medium base bulb (not included). On-off in-line switch on the cord.
  • Venice modern wall lamp by 360 Lighting. Shade moves up, down, left, or right. Arm does not move.
  • Matte black and antique brass finish. Metal construction. Black metal cone shade.
